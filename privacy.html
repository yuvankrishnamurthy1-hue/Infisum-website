Privacy Policy

A short, plain explanation of what this site collects and what happens to it. Last updated July 2026.

Information we collect

The only personal information this site collects is what you choose to enter into the contact form: first name, last name, email address, organization (optional), the topic you select, and your message.

How it's used

Form submissions are used solely to respond to your enquiry. We don't add you to a mailing list or share your details with anyone outside Infinite Sum Modeling as a result of contacting us.

Third-party services

Submitting the contact form sends your information to FormSubmit.co, a third-party form-delivery service, which forwards it to our inbox by email. This site's typefaces are loaded from Google Fonts, which involves a request to Google's servers when the page loads.

Analytics and cookies

As of this writing, this site does not use analytics, tracking cookies, or advertising pixels of any kind.

Data retention

We keep messages sent through the contact form only as long as needed to respond to you. If you'd like a message deleted sooner, just let us know.

Questions

For anything related to this policy or your data, email sumathi@infisum.com.
