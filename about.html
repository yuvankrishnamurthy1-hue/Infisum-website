Our story

Why Infinite Sum.

Better decisions combine evidence, expertise and context.

The name comes from the Isopanishad: infinite value stays whole no matter how much is added or taken away. That's roughly how we think about the work too. Collaboration over competition, and every engagement should leave everyone involved a little better off than before.

In practice, that means economic analysis, custom CGE, econometric and financial models, specialized databases, forecasting, training, and advice on emerging technology, whether that's data science, AI, blockchain or robotics.

∞
Value that only compounds.
Every engagement adds to a shared body of models, data and insight — and none of it diminishes.
