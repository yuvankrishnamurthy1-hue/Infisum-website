Home/Capabilities

Methods built for hard decisions.

Economic modelling, supply-chain analysis, data science and policy strategy.

Capability 01

Economic Modeling

We build Computable General Equilibrium models that trace how a single policy decision, a tariff, a subsidy, a trade agreement, ripples through every sector of an economy.

  • Trade & investment liberalization impact
  • Subsidy & customs-duty structure analysis
  • Long-run growth & development scenarios
Capability 02

Supply Chain Analytics

We map global value chains end to end, stress-test them against disruption, and work out the real trade-offs between cost, resilience and emissions.

  • Cross-border flow & bottleneck modelling
  • Resilience & risk quantification
  • Network optimisation at scale
Capability 03

Data Science · AI / ML

Machine learning grounded in economic theory: forecasting, causal inference and automation pipelines that turn messy institutional data into decisions.

  • Predictive & causal modelling
  • Large-scale data engineering
  • Applied AI for research workflows
Capability 04

Strategic Policy Analysis

We assess how disruptive technologies reshape markets and mandates, so governments and enterprises know what's coming before it arrives.

  • Disruptive-technology impact studies
  • Business & public-policy strategy
  • Digital-transmission & MSME analysis
